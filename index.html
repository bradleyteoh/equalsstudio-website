About

Who we are

Equals Design Studio is a Malaysia-based interior design and renovation practice, founded by Cayden Teoh and Trisha Teoh. We work with corporate offices, retail environments, and residential clients across the Klang Valley and beyond — translating briefs into spaces that are functional, considered, and built to last.

Our team handles design, on-site project management, and client liaison end-to-end. Past and ongoing work spans corporate offices, supermarkets, shopping mall fit-outs, and private homes.

Services

What we do

Commercial

Corporate offices, supermarkets, shopping malls, and large-format retail. Full design and project management from concept to handover.

Residential

Homes and condominiums designed around how you live — practical, personal, and well-crafted.

Renovation

Fit-outs, refurbishments, and space upgrades. We manage contractors, materials, and timelines so you don't have to.
